Bungee Spice
Bungee Spice is a derivative of David Jonathan Ross font Bungee.
Bungee Spice is a COLRv1 font exhibiting multiple gradients within
each glyph. Bungee Spice is released under the SIL Open Font License.

Croissant One
Croissant is a typeface inspired by the Parisian spirit, in the people and
the landscapes there. The lowercase letters have smooth round shapes,
and a nice long out-stroke to connect nearly every glyph, reminding the reader
of elegant French handwriting. The uppercases have a classical structure and
soft terminals that embody the spirit of this multi-purpose typeface.

Pixelify Sans
Pixelify Sans fonts are a unique style of typography that originated
in the 1980s with the rise of computer graphics and video games.
Pixel fonts are characterized by their blocky, pixelated appearance,
which is achieved by using a grid of small, square pixels to create each
letterform.
